# Secret Weimar

Secret Weimar is a fun and innovative twist on Secret Hitler which aims to expand on the Secret Hitler universe and introduce therein multiple parties and all the chaos and fun that it leads to. The game follows a trajectory similar to secret hitler, in that it starts with a rotating Chancellery and the typical voting, but the rules have been expanded to support multiple parties, all of whom are still in a two coalition system in keeping with the essence of the original game. Each party however knows a differing set of information points and has to do what is best for their alliance on the basis of their gathered information.
